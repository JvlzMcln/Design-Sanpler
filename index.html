Bio

My design journey began in 1999, evolving from illustration, print, and graphic design to web design, UI/UX, and Product Design. I have navigated various roles within start-ups and collaborated with esteemed companies like Macy’s, Captain Morgan, and American Airlines, honing my craft and gaining valuable insights.
Passionate about design’s transformative potential, I am committed to continuous learning and evolution. I am particularly interested in the innovative applications of UI/UX in industries such as Energy, Healthcare, and Finance, where its impact is profound. Embarking on this redirection in my design career is an exhilarating journey. As a lifelong student of design, I am prepared to tackle new challenges and make meaningful contributions. I am enthusiastic about the possibility of joining your team and am keen to discuss how we can collaborate to achieve mutual success.
I strongly believe in the transformative potential of AI in design, enabling us to craft more intuitive and compelling user experiences. By embracing these technologies, we can push the boundaries of creativity and innovation, leading to more impactful and user-centric solutions. I’m excited about contributing to projects that explore the forefront of AI-driven design and collaborating with like-minded individuals to push the boundaries of what’s possible in design.
